### Healthy Travel Surrey # Helping Surrey take confident, publicly supported action on transport, health and place

Healthy Travel Surrey exists to help Surrey’s decision‑makers and community organisations take practical, evidence‑led action that improves everyday travel, strengthens local economies and supports healthy, thriving places.

Read the Surrey Transport Pledge Start a conversation
--- ## Why we exist {#why-we-exist} Local leadership on transport is harder than it should be: inbox bombardment, aggressive opposition, safety concerns, gaps in local economic evidence, and memories of poorly delivered schemes all push leaders toward caution over action. We change that dynamic. We’re calm, professional, non‑partisan, and evidence‑led. We don’t ask individuals to stand alone; we help convene power, align stakeholders and build the shared case for investment and reform. With up‑to‑date data and narrative around health, prosperity and placemaking, we help absorb conflict and keep debate grounded in common values. ### Mission We’ve come together to help Surrey’s decision‑makers and community‑based organisations take confidence‑backed, publicly supported action that positions the county to thrive. --- ## What we do {#what-we-do}
### Reframe the narrative - Health - Safety - Prosperity - Place ### Evidence synthesis - Air quality - Health - High street vitality - Safety - Local economic data ### Stakeholder alignment - Community - Health - Business - Schools - Accessibility advocates ### Conflict absorption - Maintain constructive, proportionate debate - Avoid polarising frames ### Delivery support - Focus on what works - What’s fundable - What residents will support
--- ## Who we work with {#who-we-work-with} We collaborate with: - Councillors & MPs - Community & place‑based organisations - Health & wellbeing groups - Business forums & town centres - Accessibility & disability advocates - Schools, parents & young people - Regional & national partners --- ## Our approach {#our-approach} 1. **Listening:** Build relationships, understand concerns and lived reality. 2. **Visibility:** Make alignment visible to decision‑makers, residents and media. 3. **Credibility:** Borrow from the best, use recognised evidence, link to national policy. 4. **Influence:** Help shape delivery, funding and communications. --- ## How we’ll deliver a better transport future for Surrey {#priorities} Our strategic priorities fully support, and are designed to complement, the direction set out in Surrey County Council’s Local Transport Plan (LTP4). LTP4 provides a strong long‑term framework; our focus is on helping create the conditions for delivery by bringing evidence, alignment and community support to the table. [Read LTP4](https://www.surreycc.gov.uk/roads-and-transport/policies-plans-consultations/transport-plan){: target="_blank" } --- ## Strategic Priorities {#strategic-priorities} ### 1. Cleaner Air & Climate‑Resilient Travel - Reduce harmful pollution around homes, schools and workplaces. - Support walking, cycling and wheeling for short trips. - Make it easier to switch to electric vehicles. - Plan for climate resilience and flooding. **What this means for you:** Healthier air for local streets and a county prepared for the future. --- ### 2. Reliable, Affordable and Desirable Public Transport - Improve bus reliability, frequency and access to key services. - Make train stations more accessible. - Improve interchanges. - Support community transport providers. - Reduce travel costs. **What this means for you:** Transport you can actually plan your day around. --- ### 3. Safer Streets for Everyone - Build safe, welcoming active travel infrastructure. - Improve crossings, school parking and junctions. - Support 20mph zones. - Reduce fear and intimidation from traffic. - Support inclusive design. **What this means for you:** Calmer, more welcoming streets. --- ### 4. Stronger Local Communities - Plan new development around good transport. - Help high streets thrive. - Support community‑led placemaking. - Improve access to jobs and services. - Reduce isolation. **What this means for you:** Thriving high streets, shorter trips and better access to services. --- ### 5. Smarter, Better Managed Roads - Reduce disruption and bottlenecks. - Improve safety and reduce speeding. - Support EV infrastructure. - Reduce rat‑running and congestion. **What this means for you:** Smoother journeys and roads that work better for everyone. --- ## The Surrey Transport Pledge {#surrey-transport-pledge} Our strategic priorities fully support the direction set by Surrey County Council’s Local Transport Plan (LTP4).
